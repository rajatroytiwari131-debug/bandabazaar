 Customer Panel (the app/website your buyers use)

Must-Have Features:



* Location detection (auto-detect using GPS or enter pincode)

* Browse by category (Vegetables, Dairy, Snacks, Medicines, etc.)

* Search with filters (brand, price, in-stock only)

* Product detail pages with clear photos and prices

* Cart with real-time stock validation

* Multiple payment options — UPI, card, cash on delivery

* Order tracking with live status (Acce

* Keep the homepage simple — show nearby stores and popular categories first

* Display delivery time estimates prominently (e.g., "Delivered in 25 min")

* Use large product images — poor photos kill conversions

* One-tap reorder for repeat customers

* Push notifications for order status updates

🏪 Store Panel (dashboard for your partner shop owners)

Must-Have Features:



* Simple login — OTP-based, no complicated passwords

* Order notification with loud alert (many shopkeepers miss silent pings)

* Accept / Reject order button with reason selection

* Order details — item list, customer address, payment mode

* Inventory management — add products, update stock, mark out-of-stock

* Bulk product upload via Excel/CSV sheet

* Earnings dashboard — daily, weekly, monthly sales

* Settlement reports — when and how much they'll receive

* Delivery assignment — assign to their own delivery boy or self-deliver

* Working hours setting (open/close store toggle)

UX Best Practices:



* The store panel must work well on a basic Android phone — most small shopkeepers won't use a laptop

* Auto-print order receipt if they have a Bluetooth thermal printer

* Keep the UI in Hindi as well as English — critical for local adoption

* Train shopkeepers with a short 10-minute video walkthrough, not a manual

⚙️ Admin Panel (your control center)

Must-Have Features:



* Dashboard overview — orders today, active stores, revenue, failed deliveries

* Store onboarding — approve/reject store applications, verify documents (GST, FSSAI if applicable)

* Product catalog management — master list, category creation, image standards

* Order management — view all orders, intervene if stuck, refund processing

* Customer management — view profiles, handle complaints, issue credits

* Commission settings — set per-store or per-category commission rates

* Promotional tools — create offers, banner ads, flash sales

* Analytics — bestselling products, peak order times, drop-off points in checkout

* Payout management — approve weekly settlements to store owners

* Complaint and dispute resolution queue
